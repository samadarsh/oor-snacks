Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Oor Snacks respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our website and place orders.

Information we collect

When you check out, you provide your name and delivery address to compile a WhatsApp order message. Order details may be stored locally in your browser for demo staff-queue features until you clear site data.

How we use information

We use your details solely to fulfill snack orders, communicate delivery updates, and improve our service. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Cookies & local storage

We use browser local storage to remember your shopping basket between visits on the same device.
