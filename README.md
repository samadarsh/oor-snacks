# 🌾 Oor Snacks

Traditional Tamil Nadu snacks crafted for the modern connoisseur. Oor Snacks brings you the authentic, rich culinary heritage of South India, handmade in micro-batches and delivered straight to your door with pristine freshness. 🥣

---

## 🏡 Our Story & Philosophy

At Oor (meaning "hometown" or "roots" in Tamil), we believe that traditional snacks are more than just food—they are time machines. Our recipes are sourced from heritage kitchens across Tamil Nadu, prepared by master artisans who have kept these culinary arts alive for generations.

We are committed to absolute purity:
- 🥜 **Premium Oils Only**: Fried exclusively in cold-pressed groundnut oil and pure ghee. No palm oil, no vegetable oil blends, and no reused fats.
- 🍳 **Micro-Batch Crafting**: We prepare our snacks in small, dedicated batches to ensure every single bite retains the crispness, aroma, and delicate spice balance of home cooking.
- 💨 **Aroma-Lock Freshness**: Packed immediately in specialized matte pouches to guard against moisture and oxygen, locking in that freshly-fried fragrance until you break the seal.

## 📦 The Collection

Our menu is a curated celebration of Tamil Nadu's finest savories and sweets:

### 🌶️ Signature Savories
- **Traditional Kai Murukku**: Crisp, golden rice flour coils, hand-twisted with precision, flavored with cumin, butter, and black sesame.
- **Spicy Garlic Karasev**: Bold, crunchy chickpea flour sticks spiced with crushed black pepper, red chilies, and fresh garlic.
- **Classic Madras Mixture**: The ultimate tea-time snack—a harmonious blend of fine omapodi, crunchy boondi, ribbon pakoda, toasted cashews, and fried curry leaves.

### 🍯 Iconic Sweets
- **Tirunelveli Halwa**: Slow-cooked wheat halwa prepared in pure ghee with chopped cashews, offering a rich, melt-in-your-mouth texture.

### 🎁 Gifting & Curated Boxes
- **Classic Tea-Time Box**: A curated assortment of savories and banana chips, perfect for your daily coffee or tea pairings.
- **Traditional Festive Gifting Box**: A premium selection of heritage sweets and savories, housed in an elegant terracotta and gold package designed for sharing on special occasions.
