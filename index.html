About Oor

ஊர் — the taste of home

Handcrafted cultural craftsmanship

Oor is not a snack catalogue. It is a Chennai kitchen tradition carried forward — stone-ground flours, cold-pressed oils, and recipes shaped by gatherings, festivals, and everyday tea-time rituals.

We honour the pace of real preparation: limited batches, no palm oil, no industrial shortcuts. Every pack is meant to arrive with the warmth and crunch of something made hours ago, not months ago.

Origin

குடும்ப சமையல் · FAMILY KITCHENS

Inspired before it was a brand

Oor began in the quiet rhythm of home — murukku twisted by hand, oil measured by memory, laughter between batches cooling on the counter.

Long before there was a label or a website, there were recipes that filled family gatherings. We built Oor to share that same care with homes across India — not as a factory story, but as an emotional origin story rooted in Mylapore, Chennai.
