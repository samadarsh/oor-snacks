Our Craft

From Dough to Crunch

A step-by-step glimpse into traditional Tamil snack preparation.

Step 01

The Ponni Rice

We begin with raw Ponni rice and roasted urad dal, stone-ground in village mills to preserve natural starches and ensure a dry, crispy texture with minimal oil retention.

Step 02

Hand-Rolled Craft

Our experienced local homemakers coil and twist the fresh dough by hand, ring-by-ring, on clean wooden boards. This gentle handling lets the dough breathe for the perfect density.

Step 03

Wood-Fired Hearth

The dough rings are dropped gently into boiling wood-pressed groundnut oil, heating in vintage brass cauldrons over firewood coals to produce deep, wood-fired caramelization.

Step 04

The Fresh Crunch

Emerging golden-crisp, seasoned with fresh butter, black cumin, and sea salt, each batch is tasted to ensure a clean, crunching bite with zero heavy aftertaste.

Step 05

Sealed for You

Packed immediately into our premium double-walled nitrogen-flushed pouches, shielding the crisp freshness from light and moisture until you open it.
