Our Craft

The Element of Fire

“Fire is not merely a heat source. It is an active ingredient, a volatile partner, and the ultimate arbiter of flavor.”

At Ignis, we return to the primal origins of cooking, refined through modern culinary gastronomy. We harvest local oak, hickory, and fruitwood to orchestrate distinct temperature zones, matching each timber's smoke profile to the specific protein or vegetable it kisses.

The Visionary

Chef Marcus Vance

With over two decades spent in wood-fired hearth cooking across the Mediterranean and Northern California, Chef Marcus Vance co-founded Ignis to push the boundaries of elemental cuisine.

“Fire is unpredictable. Controlling it requires an intimate dialogue between the chef, the logs, the air, and the pan. We are not just cooking; we are staging a performance of precision and temperature.”

Michelin Star Recipient Best New Chef — Culinary Review

“An absolute masterclass in flavor extraction. The wood-fire notes are incredibly delicate, never overwhelming, framing the ingredients with unmatched precision.”

Sarah Jenkins, San Francisco Chronicle
